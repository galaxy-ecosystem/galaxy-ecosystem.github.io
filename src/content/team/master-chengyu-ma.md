Chengyu Ma is a M.Sc. student in astrophysics at the University of Science and Technology of China, advised by Prof. Enci Wang. He received his B.Sc. in Physics from Zhengzhou University in 2024.

His research focuses on star formation and stellar feedback on the multiphase interstellar medium in various galaxies. He works with cosmological simulations, utilizing data from the IllustrisTNG simulation.
