Zeyu Chen is a Ph.D. student in astrophysics at the University of Science and Technology of China, advised by Prof. Enci Wang. He received his B.Sc. in Astrophysics from USTC in 2024.

His research focuses on the circumgalactic medium, quasar absorption lines, galaxy formation and evolution, stellar feedback, and the baryon cycle. He works with large spectroscopic and imaging surveys including DESI and JWST/JADES.

Zeyu's recent work includes first-author studies on the cosmic evolution and spatial distribution of multiphase gas associated with QSOs, and on Mg II absorption in the circumgalactic medium with DESI.
